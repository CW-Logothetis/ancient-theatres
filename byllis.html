How to get here

FROM TIRANA

The highway from Athens to Corinth (tolls) is the best option and from there you have two choices:

  • Coastal road (10) to Palaia Epidavros and then road 70 to the ancient city
  • Visit ancient Corinth and then take the old Corinth-Argos road that passes Ancient Mycenae (a must visit). Consider stops in Argos and Nafplio (modern Greece’s first capital) before joining road 70 to Ancient Epidavros.
